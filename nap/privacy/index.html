Nap: Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21st, 2021
Versions: 2.3 and later

Nap Application

João Pedro da Cunha e Silva Martins Costa (Leao) created and develops Nap (the Application).

This page lists the policies regarding collection, use, and disclosure of information of users of the Application.

Information gathering and usage

Regarding collection, use, and disclosure of information, the Application:

  • Does not have Internet access;
  • Does not require, collect, or track any identifiable or personal information;
  • Does not have ads;
  • Uses Google Play Services to process purchases, backup Sensitive Data, and collect Error Data. These services may collect information used to identify the user. Make sure to review Google Privacy Policy.
Sensitive Data

The Application stores data of your notifications and applications installed in your device. This data is called Sensitive Data.

Sensitive Data is stored locally on your device. The Application supports Android's Auto Backup feature. Auto Backup may automatically back up Sensitive Data, by uploading it to your Google Drive, where it is protected by your Google Account credentials.

Before the Application can read any notifications, you must grant it access in Android's Notification Access page. You can revoke this access at any given time. Android's Notification Access settings are also accessible through the Application's settings page.

Error Data

The Application collects data for handled and unhandled errors (crashes). This data is called Error Data.

Handled errors are stored in a simple file only accessible by the Application.

Unhandled errors are collected by Google Play Services automatically. Their data may include information such as your device’s Internet Protocol address, name, and operating system version. Furthermore, it may include information about the Application and its usage, such as configuration and time and date of the error, among other statistics.

Security

Sensitive Data is stored locally on your device's internal storage and is accessible only to your application. The Android system implements this protection, and it is sufficient for most applications.

Google Play Services transfer data via Internet standard protocols and employ multiple layers of network devices and intrusion detection to protect their external attack surface.

Links to other websites

The Application may contain links that direct to third-party websites. Third-party websites are not operated by Leao and you should always review their Privacy Policy. Leao has no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy might change in the future. All changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately after they are posted. If substantial changes are made, you may receive a notification in the Application. Otherwise, if you want to be up to date on the way your information is handled, you should review leao.io/nap/privacy periodically.

Contact Leao

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact Leao at info@leao.io.
